Our Beneficiaries

Migrant Workers' Assistance Fund

Amount donated: Currently raising funds

The Migrant Workers' Assistance Fund (MWFA) is a humanitarian charity of The Migrant Worker Centre (MWC).
They address employment disputes and increases awareness among migrant workers of their employment rights.They also help promote migrant workers' social integration into our society.

Donate more now

The Courage Fund

Amount donated: $252.45

This fund provides relief and assistance to those in need. A lump sum of up to $1000 is given to vulnerable families, including healthcare and frontline workers
